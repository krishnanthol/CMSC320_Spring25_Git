Q1: Why might we consider using Git?
Answer: We might consider using Git to maintain history of a codebase over time, develop features in isolation
to maintain stability, easily facilitate collaboration, and/or perform code reviews.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.
Answer: Data science could be applied to e-commerce to gauge user behavior, predict trends, and test the
effectiveness of new features.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?
Answer: Merging combines the content from two branches into one, whereas rebasing moves content from one 
branch to another.
